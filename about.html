About KinConnected

KinConnected was born from a deeply personal journey—one that revealed just how many people are walking through life missing key pieces of their family story. Whether due to adoption, family secrets, or generational loss, too many of us are left with unanswered questions about where we come from.

We believe that everyone deserves to know their roots. Our mission is to help individuals reconnect with the branches of their family tree through personalized, respectful, and detail-driven genealogical research. Using DNA analysis, historical records, and compassionate investigation, we walk with you toward clarity, healing, and understanding.

This work isn’t just about names and dates—it’s about people, identity, and legacy. Whether you're seeking to identify a biological parent, expand a family tree, or simply make sense of your story, we're here to help you uncover what’s been waiting to be found.

Because we deserve to know.
